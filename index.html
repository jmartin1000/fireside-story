Flashback!

Ah! It's coming back to me. It was NOT another stormy night. In fact, it was a beautiful fall day, and I was at a local festival. It was a bike festival, which is why I was riding my bike. But I was in disguise. Already at my young age, I knew the danger of them spotting me. I literally dashed into her. She was riding a pink mountain bike, but when I ran into her, I knocked her right off that bike and into the grass beside the pavement.

She had been wearing a backpack and balancing a water bottle on her head. And as strange as that seems, the even stranger part was that when she stood up after her tumble, her backpack was on the ground beside the bike, but her water bottle was still balanced on her head.

While I gawked, she collected her backpack, remounted her bike, and promptly pedalled off. The water bottle never fell off her head.

Since that time, I have never seen her without that water bottle. Perhaps it is more accurate to say that I have never seen that water bottle without her. Until yesterday. When we saw her water bottle in the parking lot, with no sign of her, we knew that something was up.
